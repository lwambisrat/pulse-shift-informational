Greens Mtaani Documentation

Greens Mtaani is a digital platform designed to empower informal grocery vendors in Kenya, known as **Mama Mboga**, by connecting them with tech-savvy customers, particularly the youth. The platform bridges the gap between traditional vendors and modern consumers through a dual-application approach. It aims to enhance vendor efficiency, boost sales, reduce food waste, and promote local economic participation by expanding market access for Mama Mboga.

Greens Mtaani Nutrition Planning Feature

The Nutrition Planning feature in Greens Mtaani helps you create personalized meal plans tailored to your health goals, allergies, and meal preferences. You can view meal recipes, cooking steps, and generate grocery lists to make healthy eating easy and convenient.

1. Nutrition Home Screen

What it does:

This screen welcomes you to the nutrition feature. You can either auto-generate a meal plan based on your inputs or manually create a custom meal plan.

How to use:

  • Tap Auto-Generate Meal Plan to quickly get a recommended daily meal plan.
  • Tap Custom Meal Planning to build your meals one by one.

2. Goal Selection Screen

Purpose:

Here, you select your health or fitness goal. This helps Greens Mtaani customize your meal plan to match your objective.

Options include:

  • Lose Weight
  • Gain Weight
  • Get in Shape
  • Eat Healthier
  • Maintain Weight

How to use:

  • Choose the goal that best fits your needs.
  • Tap Next to continue.

3. Allergies Selection Screen

Purpose:

To ensure your meal plan is safe, tell us if you have any allergies.

Common allergies listed:

  • Peanuts
  • Dairy
  • Broccoli
  • Fish
  • Other

How to use:

  • Check all allergies that apply to you.
  • Tap Next after selecting.

4. Meal Frequency Screen

Purpose:

Select how many meals you want per day so your plan can be adjusted accordingly.

Options:

  • 3 Meals/Day
  • 4 Meals/Day
  • 5 Meals/Day

How to use:

  • Pick the number of meals you prefer each day.
  • Tap Generate Meal Plan to see your personalized meals.

5. Meal Plan Overview Screen

Purpose:

View your daily meals for breakfast, lunch, dinner, and snacks along with pictures.

How to use:

  • Tap the days of the week to view meal plans for different days.
  • Click Refresh on any meal to get an alternative suggestion.

6. Recipe Ingredients Screen

Purpose:

See the exact ingredients needed for each recipe.

How to use:

  • Review the ingredient list for the chosen meal.
  • Tap Get Groceries to add these items to your shopping list.

7. Recipe Procedure Screen

Purpose:

Get step-by-step cooking instructions for each recipe.

How to use:

  • Follow the numbered steps to prepare your meal.
  • Switch between Ingredients and Procedures tabs for reference.
  • Tap Get Groceries if you want to add ingredients to your list.

8. Grocery List Screen

Purpose:

Shows you a categorized grocery list based on your meal plan to make shopping easy.

Categories include:

  • Vegetables
  • Fruits
  • Others as needed

How to use:

  • Check off items you have or have purchased.
  • Adjust quantities if needed before shopping.
